My name is Joshua Kornmayer. I'm a recent graduate of Appalachian State Univeristy with a Bachelor's of Science degree in Mathematics and Electronic Media/Broadcasting. It's an odd pairing being a broadcaster and mathematician, but it's the best of both worlds! I love sports, stats, and math problems!

Since becoming a broadcaster I have had so many opportunities in the broadcasting field. I have called ESPN broadcasts, the very first Sunbelt Football Championship Game, and have been the voice of Appalachian State Women's Basketball. I've hosted my own morning show, and it got recognized as one of the top 5 college morning shows in the nation.
